Venue information

The 2018 workshop will take place in Honolulu, Hawaii, on the island of Oahu. The main event will be hosted at the Ala Moana Hotel:

  • Ala Moana Hotel
  • 410 Atkinson Drive, Honolulu, Hawaii 96814
  • Phone: 808-955-4811 | Fax: 808-944-6839
