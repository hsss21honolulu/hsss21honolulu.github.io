Special Session: Women under pressure

Women under pressure session, which is open to all participants (male and female), will present the obstacles and solutions in closing the gender gap in STEM. In this session we are providing some information about resources that we have put together in an effort to help our community in promoting a balanced and progressive environment where everyone can thrive and looking forward to hearing your feedback and suggestions.
