Session 9: Computational approaches in extreme conditions science

Computational approaches such as ab initio calculations have advanced considerably over the last decade and now play an integral and indispensable role in extreme-conditions science. Session 10 will focus on computational studies of novel high-pressure phases, phase diagrams, and high-pressure and -temperature properties of condensed matter.
